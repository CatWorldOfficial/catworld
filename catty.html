🗣️Decoding Feline Language

Understanding your cat's communication can greatly improve your relationship with them. Here are some key signals to look out for:

Common Sounds

Short Meow: Greeting. Purr: Contentment. Hiss: Warning. New: Trills/Chirrups are friendly greetings; Chattering happens when watching prey.

Body Language

Slow Blink: Trust. Tail Up: Friendly. New: Flat whiskers signal fear. Piloerection (puffed fur) means they are terrified.

Micro-Expressions

Flehmen Response: Opening the mouth to "taste-smell" scents. Muzzle Tension: A tight mouth can signal pain.

Pro Tips

Blink Back: Confirm trust. The Venus Trap: A belly flash is trust, but often a defensive "ready to fight" pose.

💖Care Hub

01. Advanced Nutrition +

Cats require taurine, an essential amino acid. Always choose high-quality animal protein over plant-based fillers.

02. Hydration Tips +

Many cats don't drink enough. Try a water fountain or adding wet food to their diet to increase liquid intake.

03. Dental Health +

Dental disease is common. Regular brushing or dental treats can prevent costly vet visits and tooth loss.
